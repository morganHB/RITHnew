Who we are

RITH Professional Services was founded in 2019 after the closure of PriceWaterhouseCoopers in Welkom. Under the leadership of Chartered Accountant Rian van Schalkwyk, our boutique firm has flourished to offer a comprehensive range of services including accounting, tax, secretarial, estates and advisory.

We view every client relationship as a partnership and genuinely believe that our success comes with yours. Our core values include respect, integrity, trust and honesty. These pillars underpin everything we do.
