About Us

Professional Services

We proudly offer professional services rendered by a team of highly qualified professionals. RITH was founded in 2019 after the closure of PriceWaterhouseCoopers in Welkom. Under the leadership of Chartered Accountant and Director Rian van Schalkwyk, the team has flourished and now renders services like accounting, tax, secretarial, estates and advisory services.

Our boutique firm views every client relationship as a partnership, and we genuinely believe that our success only comes with your success. We strive to exceed expectations and take you beyond the numbers.

Feel free to drop in for a visit at our offices located at 68 Prospero Street, Welkom. We would be delighted to meet you and discuss how we can support your business.
