Our Services

Our services are carefully designed to support your business at every stage. We are also an accredited SAIPA training centre.

Accounting

  • Compilation and preparation of annual financial statements (ISRS 4410/IFRS for SME/IFRS).
  • Compilation of working papers and working files.
  • Monthly accounting services and payroll services.
  • Preparation of monthly management accounts and VAT services.
  • Training of staff.

Tax

  • Application and issuing of Tax Compliance Status Pins (Letter of Good Standing).
  • Application and issuing of Tax Directives (IRP5e) and Foreign Investment Allowances (FIA).
  • Calculation, completion and submission of employees’ tax returns (EMP201) and Dividends Tax returns (DTR).
  • Completion and submission of employee tax certificates (IRP5/IT3a), provisional tax returns (IRP6) and annual income tax returns (IT12/IT14).
  • Guidance on tax laws, Tax Administration Act requirements and tax planning.
  • Training of staff.

Secretarial

  • Corporate governance and company secretarial services.
  • CIPC registrations, directorate changes and lodgements.
  • Assistance with the requirements and implementation of the Companies Act.
  • Allotment, acquisition and transfer of shares; amendments of authorised and issued share capital.
  • Trust administration, drafting new trust deeds and amendments as required by the Trust Property Control Act.
  • Legal agreements and alterations on Memorandum of Incorporation and shareholders agreements.

Estates

  • Estate planning tailored to your needs.
  • Drafting of modern wills, including wills incorporating discretionary testamentary trusts.
  • Estate administration and winding up of deceased estates as executors or as agents of nominated executors.

Advisory

  • Structuring of business and group structures.
  • Business valuations and guidance on buying or selling a business.
  • Advice on and preparation of shareholder agreements.
  • Preparation of cash flow projections and tailored financial advice.
